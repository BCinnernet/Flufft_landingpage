Welcome to FLUFFT

FLUFFT is a handmade rug and textile art brand inspired by creativity, nostalgia, culture, and the freedom of play. Made for eclectic spaces, creative minds, and the inner child that never really left. Every design is created to spark curiosity, comfort, and conversation. We blend tactile art, vibrant storytelling, and playful imagination into pieces that invite people to slow down, reconnect, and experience creativity through touch.
